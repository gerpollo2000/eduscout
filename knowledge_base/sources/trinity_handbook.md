# Trinity School — Family Handbook 2025-2026

## About Trinity
Trinity School, founded in 1709, is the oldest continuously operating school in New York City. It is a K-12 independent, college-preparatory school located on the Upper West Side. Trinity consistently ranks among the top private schools in the nation.

## Admission
- Application via Ravenna platform
- Application fee: $75
- Required: student visit, parent interview, teacher recommendations (2)
- Grades K-4: ERB/ISEE testing
- Grades 5-12: ISEE or SSAT scores
- Financial aid applications via SSS (School and Student Services)
- Application deadline: December 1 for following fall

## Tuition (2025-2026)
- Kindergarten: $59,270/year
- Grades 1-4: $60,180/year
- Grades 5-8: $62,350/year
- Grades 9-12: $63,900/year
- Registration deposit: $5,000 (applied to tuition)
- Lunch: Included in tuition (catered, nutritionist-supervised)
- Textbooks and technology: Included

## Financial Aid
- 22% of students receive financial aid
- Need-based awards ranging from $5,000 to full tuition
- Average grant: $42,000/year
- Apply through SSS by January 15

## Special Needs Accommodations

### ADHD
- Learning specialists on staff (4 full-time)
- Extended time on tests available with documentation
- Preferential seating
- Check-in system with advisory teachers
- On-site psychologist for consultation
- Study skills workshops (optional, included)

### Dyslexia
- Wilson Reading and Orton-Gillingham trained tutors
- Assistive technology (Kurzweil, Learning Ally)
- Modified testing accommodations

### Physical Accessibility
- Main campus building (139 W 91st St) has limited elevator access
- Upper School building (101 W 91st St) is fully accessible
- ADA accommodations provided on case-by-case basis
- Contact ADA coordinator for specific needs: ada@trinityschoolnyc.org

Note: The main K-4 building has architectural limitations due to its historic designation. Families with wheelchair needs should contact admissions to discuss specific accommodations available at each campus.

## Dress Code
Trinity has a dress code (not a full uniform):
- Collared shirts or blouses required (grades 5-12)
- No jeans, athletic wear, or flip-flops during school hours
- K-4: Clean, neat, weather-appropriate clothing
- School spirit wear permitted on Fridays
- Religious headcoverings always permitted

## Academic Programs
- AP courses: 22 available
- Class size: Average 16 students

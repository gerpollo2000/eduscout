# Success Academy Harlem — Family Handbook 2025-2026

## About Success Academy
Success Academy Harlem is a tuition-free public charter school serving grades K-12 in Central Harlem. Part of the Success Academy Charter Schools network, it emphasizes academic rigor, structured environment, and high expectations for all students.

## Admission
- Open enrollment via lottery (no test required)
- Application through NYC Charter School Center
- Lottery held in April for following school year
- Siblings of current students receive priority
- Open to all NYC residents
- No tuition — fully funded by public funding

## Tuition and Fees
- Tuition: Free (public charter school)
- Supplies: Provided by school
- Uniforms: Required (family purchases, approximately $50-75/year)
- Field trips: Included, no additional cost

## Uniform Policy
SUCCESS ACADEMY HAS A MANDATORY UNIFORM:
- Orange polo shirt with Success Academy logo (purchased through school vendor)
- Khaki or navy pants/skirts
- Black or brown belt required (grades 3+)
- Black, white, or navy socks
- Black shoes (no open toes, no sneakers except during PE)
- Cold weather: Navy or black sweater/cardigan (no hoodies)
- PE days: School-issued PE uniform
- Violations: First offense = warning, second = parent notification, third = student sent home

## Special Needs & Accessibility

### ADHD Support
- Behavioral support team in every school
- Structured daily schedule with clear expectations
- Positive reinforcement systems (merit/demerit tracking)
- Small-group tutoring available
- School psychologist on site
- IEP and 504 plan coordination with NYC DOE

### Physical Accessibility
- Building is wheelchair accessible
- Elevator available
- Accessible restrooms
- IEP transportation services coordinated through NYC DOE

### Autism Support
- ICT (Integrated Co-Teaching) classes available
- SETSS (Special Education Teacher Support Services) provided
- Behavioral intervention plans
- Social-emotional learning curriculum embedded in all grades

## Academic Approach
- Extended school day: 7:45 AM - 4:00 PM
- Saturday Academy (optional, test prep)
- STEM-focused curriculum
- Daily reading period (DEAR time)
- Chess integrated into curriculum (K-5)
- Science labs starting in kindergarten

## Attendance Policy
STRICT ATTENDANCE POLICY:
- 95% attendance rate expected
- 3 consecutive unexcused absences = mandatory parent conference
- Chronic absenteeism (10%+ absences) may affect enrollment for following year
- Late arrival policy: Doors close at 7:50 AM, students arriving after marked tardy

## Discipline Policy
- Merit/demerit system
- Community detention for repeated minor infractions
- Suspension for serious offenses
- Restorative circles used for conflict resolution
- Zero tolerance for: weapons, drugs, fighting, bullying

## Transportation
- Free yellow school bus for students in zones (K-5)
- Free MTA MetroCard for students in grades 6+
- Bus routes cover: Central Harlem, East Harlem, South Bronx
